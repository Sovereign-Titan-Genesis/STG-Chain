# STG-Chain Tokenomics (Draft)

## Supply & Distribusi
- Total supply: 1.000.000.000 STG
- Distribusi awal:
  - 40% komunitas & contributor
  - 20% validator & staking pool
  - 20% treasury & governance
  - 10% tim inti & developer
  - 10% cadangan ekosistem

## Reward Formula
- Staking reward:
  R_stake = (Inflasi tahunan * Stake validator) / Total stake
- Attestation reward: 0.1 STG per VC valid
- Reputasi multiplier: reputasi tinggi memberi bonus reward

## Inflasi & Simulasi
- Inflasi awal: 5% per tahun
- Distribusi inflasi:
  - 70% staking reward
  - 20% attestation reward
  - 10% treasury
- Simulasi 5 tahun: ±1.28 miliar STG

## Vesting & Lockup
- Tim inti: vesting 3 tahun
- Validator awal: lockup 1 tahun
- Airdrop komunitas: distribusi bertahap
